    Meet Barnes-Jones Consulting

    Barnes Jones

    Founder

    Dr. Josias Barnes-Jones is a board-certified chiropractic radiologist based out of Atlanta, GA providing diagnostic imaging interpretation services for healthcare professionals and the legal community.


    Additionally, Dr. Barnes-Jones offers continuing education and academic services for Doctors of Chiropractic and chiropractic students.


    At Barnes-Jones Consulting, we are professional, precise, and prompt. This is our mission statement and who we are. As a board-certified chiropractor and radiologist, Dr. Barnes-Jones and his team are committed to participate in and provide quality patient-centric care focusing on addressing provider, medicolegal, and other concerns that will help guide clinical decision making.
