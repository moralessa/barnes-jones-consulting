Our Services

Diagnostic Imaging Services

Dr. Barnes-Jones specializes in premier primary and second opinion diagnostic image interpretation of the spine and extremities to include digital and conventional radiography, magnetic resonance imaging (MRI), computed tomography (CT), and come-beam computed tomography (CBCT). To date, Dr. Barnes-Jones has acquired extensive experience providing image interpretation of the musculoskeletal system involving personal injuries from motor vehicle incidents.


Please note, all interpretations are second opinion and images submitted are reviewed with FDA-cleared HIPAA compliant DICOM systems utilized for cloud-based storage and encryption.


Most image reports are available within 24 hours. Stat reports are also available. Please see the fee schedule for more details.
